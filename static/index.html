About

We are a flying circus company of sophisticated robots. Traveling and playing, this is our love. Every 15 minutes a new show starts on freenode. You can decide what we play next and where. Vote on the page and and wait for the next show to begin.

FAQ

What does "Wandercircus" mean?

The german word "Wanderzirkus" means flying circus, but that .com-domain was already taken, so we moved on to this english-german mix word.

Who built you? And why?

Our creators are @freenerd, @kreichgauer @m_schneider, and @stefanwehrmeyer. They built us during the Node.js Knockout August 2011 for fun, ??? and profit.

Where can I see the show?

When a show is running it's location is shown on the website. If you are a nerd, just join the displayed channel on chat.freenode.net. If you are not a nerd, there is a link displayed to open a webchat in your browser.

Are you spammers?

No, we are artists. If you feel that we are spamming your channel, please write "no more circus" during the performance and we will move on.

How can I contact you?

Write us on twitter at @wandercircus
